# Product-Market-Fit-Analysis-Airpure-Innovation-
AirPure Innovations is a startup responding to India’s severe air pollution crisis, where 14 cities rank among the world’s top 20 most polluted. As the company prepares to develop its air purifier, it must validate market demand and define the right product strategy before investing in production and R&amp;D.

Objective
To determine : The primary pollutants the air purifier must target
Essential product features required to stay competitive
Cities and regions with the highest market demand
Seasonal demand patterns
How R&D should align with localized pollution trends

This Project is taken from Codebasics Resume Challenge.

Tools Used :- 
Power BI
Power Query
Excel

Time Taken :- 15 Days

Result Driven:- 
The Start up company can conentrate on Both metro and non-metro cities present strong opportunities. Based on AQI trends, high-potential markets.
Based on the data it can concentrate on Mumbai, Delhi, Bengaluru, Coimbartore, Andra Pradesh, Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, Jharkand, Telngana.

The Period to concentrate on Oct- Jan of the year where AQI is high.
This period should be the primary focus for:
Product launch campaigns
Marketing push
Inventory scaling
Promotional strategies

Features - AI-based auto mode, Real time AQI, Sleep mode, Voice mode(Alexa).

Pollutants targeted - PM 2.5, PM 10, Gases like CO , NO2, O3, and other pollutants like bacteria, viruses, VOCs, pet hair , small dust particles.

Filter stages- True 3 layer Hepa with activated carbon copper filter, Pre-filter for larger particles,Activated carbon filter for gases and odors,Copper-infused antibacterial layer.

Price - 18 K to 25 K.
